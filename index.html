Security Tips
🔑

Use a Password Manager

Store unique passwords for every site. Never reuse passwords across accounts.

📱

Enable 2FA

Two-factor authentication adds a second lock — even if your password leaks, your account stays safe.

🎲

Use Passphrases

Four random words like "lamp-river-clock-tide" are both memorable and extremely hard to crack.

🚫

Avoid Personal Info

Birthdates, names, and pet names are the first things attackers try. Keep passwords random.
